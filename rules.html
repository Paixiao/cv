League Rules

Rules to abide by when competing

  1. Similar to regular online play, players play two(2) six(6) minute halfs.
  2. One(1) player MUST stream during the match, no exceptions.
  3. Quitting will result in a deduction of points and the quitting party will take a loss of 3-0 and this will show on your teams goal difference(GD).
  4. Once the match ends both players will report the final score to the admin. Any discrepencies on score reporting will be reviewed via twitch recording. If no evidence is found to declare a winner, both players will suffer the Loss.
